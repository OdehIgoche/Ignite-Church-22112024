WELCOME TO IGNITE CHURCH

We’re people just like you—passionate about Jesus, loving God, and caring for others. Ignite Church is a place where you can truly live, grow, and remember that you matter deeply to God. Our hope is for you to connect with Him in a personal, powerful way while building meaningful friendships with others here.

Jeremiah 29:11 reminds us of God’s amazing promise: “For I know the plans I have for you,” declares the Lord, “plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future.” We believe God has an incredible plan for your life, and we’re excited to see you discover it! Wherever you are on your journey, we invite you to jump in at your own pace and watch what God can do. With Him, anything is possible!

Take a look around our site to learn more about Ignite Church and how you can be part of this vibrant, life-changing community. We can’t wait to meet you!

OUR LEAD PASTORS

Peculiar and Thothi have been married for 12 years, they have three awesome daughters, Tehillah, Grace-Zoe and Mercy. Peculiar has been in Ministry since 2005 and has a diploma in Divinity at Christian Leaders Institute and a certificate from Charis Bible institute.

Peculiar and Thothi are Young Ministers with a desire to see hearts of Young people burn with a hunger for God and his presence. They are passionate about revival and desire to see young life’s being transformed by the power of God and living them with long lasting encounters.

They believe in people and in ministers and raise them up with the mind to make them greater and better. They have ministered to many young people the message of grace and have left the imprint of eternal change in the lives of many everywhere they have been.
